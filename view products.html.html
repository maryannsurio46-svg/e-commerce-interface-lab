Iced Latte

An iced latte is a cold coffee beverage made by combining shots of espresso with cold milk and ice, typically served in a tall glass. Unlike iced coffee, which uses brewed coffee, an iced latte relies on espresso for a smoother, creamier, and less acidic taste. It is often sweetened with syrups and is a popular, refreshing alternative to hot coffee.
